## Digital DC Offset Correction

![image-20241229122711845](calibration/image-20241229122711845.png)
$$
X- Y\cdot \beta z^{-1}\cdot \frac{1}{1-z^{-1}} = Y
$$
therefore
$$
\frac{Y}{X} = \frac{1-z^{-1}}{1-(1-\beta)z^{-1}}
$$
